## Tax Services

Tax planning isn't just about compliance—it's about strategy. Our tax professionals work proactively to identify opportunities, manage risk, and position your business for long-term success in an ever-changing regulatory environment.

### Corporate Tax

From C-corps to pass-throughs, we help businesses of all structures optimize their tax positions. Our approach considers the full picture: federal, state, local, and international implications of every decision.

### International Tax

Global operations create complex tax challenges. We help multinational businesses navigate transfer pricing, foreign tax credits, treaty benefits, and cross-border restructuring to minimize worldwide effective tax rates.

### Tax Controversy

When disputes arise with tax authorities, our experienced team provides representation at every level—from initial audit through appeals and litigation. We work to resolve controversies efficiently while protecting your interests.
